Calcifer's Breakfast

Calcifer’s Breakfast from Howl’s Moving Castle is an iconic American breakfast with a supernatural twist – bacon and eggs cooked with the help of a demonic fire called Calcifer.

Ingredients:

  • 3 thickly cut bacon slices
  • 6 eggs

    • Ingredients for add-ons, optional:

  • 1 large onion
  • Sourdough bread
  • 1 tsp of thyme
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Some slices of tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp red wine vinegar

Step:

  1. 1. Heat the frying pan on low heat and fry the bacon until golden brown.
  2. 2. Move the bacon to a corner of the pan and fry 6 eggs on low heat as well.
  3. 3. Serve when the eggs are fried to your preferred doneness.

    4. Optional steps:

  4. Chop and mince the onion.
  5. Heat the frying pan on medium heat and fry the minced onions till translucent. Add a few slices of tomatoes.
  6. Season with thyme, salt, and red wine vinegar and fry for 1 minute.
  7. Serve together with the eggs, bacon, tomatoes, and sourdough bread.
